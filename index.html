Campus Life

i had visited in dec 2018

At Rachana education is not so much about acquiring the three “R’s”, or about amassing knowledge, or about equipping oneself for better employment opportunities but more so about working towards refining oneself. We believe that to work at knowing, at understanding and at sensitizing oneself towards the people and the world around us is the true goal of education. For me a true Rachanite is a complete person, one who works towards making himself or herself a responsible citizen of this world and one who contributes constructively to making the world a better place to live.
